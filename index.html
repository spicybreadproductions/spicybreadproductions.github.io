Features

  • Choices with Consequences: Choose how you play Orven, and see the characters shift based on your choices.
  • Butterfly Effect: Determine your path with many branching routes, some silly and some dire.
  • Replay to find all the unique endings!
  • Enjoy original character art, background art, and soundtrack that bring the city and inhabitants of Ventreville to life.

Origin story

Ventreville: A Cure for Sorrow grew out of an original Dungeons & Dragons campaign of friends during the COVID-19 pandemic. Led by Dungeon Master and now-CEO/Creative Director Renée, the team wants the game that brought them together to also be a means of connection for others in a different medium.

Spicy Bread Productions is the company formed for the purpose of releasing Ventreville. It aims to share the story of not only these characters, but also point to the depths of human relationships and hope.
